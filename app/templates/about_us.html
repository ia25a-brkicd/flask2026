Flor'Avis

Who are we?

Flor’Avis is a Swiss brand founded by five 16-year-olds. We make handmade soaps in small batches. Local, honest, and made with passion.

Every soap is developed and produced by us by hand. We focus on quality instead of mass production and stand behind every product.

Flor’Avis is a youth project, but we take our work seriously.

Our values

Flor’Avis stands for simple, high-quality soap with a focus on natural care, clean design, and conscious consumption. For people who want quality without unnecessary additives.
